About Us

The goal for the people working at DoesItMix.co.uk is to create a impartial place where drug users can research the dangers of mixing certain drugs before taking them, where statements are based on scientific facts and not aimes at scare-mongering. We understand that people will take drugs regardless of their legality. Please use the comment secion on pages for combinations that you have tried and inform others of any dangers associated with the relevant combination.

If you have any ideas for the future of the website or any functionality you think we should add, send us an email to info@DoesItMix.co.uk.
